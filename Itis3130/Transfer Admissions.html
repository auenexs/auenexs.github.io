The Transfer Pathway

Three steps.
One clear path.

We've made the process as straightforward as possible. From checking your eligibility to submitting your application, every step is designed to get you where you're going faster.

01
Explore
Find your fit

Browse over 200 undergraduate programs and use our Transfer Equivalency System to see exactly how your community college credits will count toward your Charlotte degree.

02
Confirm
Meet the requirements

Verify your eligibility: a minimum 2.0 GPA, good academic standing at your current institution, and completion of any program-specific prerequisite courses.

03
Apply
Submit & celebrate

Complete your application through the UNC System portal. Submit official transcripts, and you may be eligible for Priority Admission if you meet our transfer agreement criteria.
