Hi, I'm Nathan Acosta

Independent STEM Tutor & Educator

I'm a passionate educator with a love for making mathematics and science accessible to everyone. My journey as a tutor started when I realized how many students were falling behind — not because they lacked ability, but because they hadn't yet found the right explanation.

Every student learns differently, and I take the time to understand how you think and where you get stuck. My goal isn't just to help you pass a test — it's to help you genuinely understand the material so you can tackle new problems confidently on your own.

Education & Background

  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics with coursework in Computer Science
  • 3+ years tutoring students from middle school through college level
  • Expertise in Math (Algebra through Calculus III), Physics, Python, and Java
  • Creator of educational content on YouTube and TikTok
  • Students consistently improve by one full letter grade or more

My Teaching Philosophy

🧠

Understanding First

I focus on deep conceptual understanding rather than rote memorization. When you truly grasp the "why," the "how" becomes much easier.

🎯

Personalized Approach

Every session is tailored to your specific needs, learning style, and goals. No two students are alike, so no two sessions are identical.

🚀

Building Confidence

Many students come to me feeling defeated. My mission is to rebuild their confidence alongside their knowledge — turning "I can't" into "I've got this."
