Cancellation Policy

Please read before booking

24-hour notice required: Cancellations made at least 24 hours before a scheduled session are fully refunded or rescheduled at no charge.

Late cancellations: Cancellations within 24 hours of the session will forfeit the session fee. Exceptions may be made for documented emergencies.

No-shows: Students who do not attend without prior notice will be charged the full session fee.

Rescheduling: You may reschedule a session up to 24 hours in advance free of charge. Contact Nathan via email or phone to reschedule.
