Project Proposal

Project Overview

Application Description & Purpose

This web application is a professional tutoring services website for my friend Nathan, an independent tutor specializing in STEM subjects. The website will serve as a central hub to introduce Nathan to prospective students and parents, outline his tutoring services, and provide an easy way to schedule sessions. The site will also feature links to Nathan's educational video content on YouTube and TikTok, extending his reach as an educator.

Intended Users

  • Students (middle school through college) seeking tutoring in Math, Physics, Python, or Java
  • Parents looking for qualified tutors for their children
  • Families interested in group tutoring sessions or package deals
  • Viewers of Nathan's social media content who want to learn more about his services

Overview of Website Content

  • Introduction to Nathan and his teaching philosophy
  • Detailed service offerings including individual and group tutoring options
  • Pricing information and package deals
  • Embedded scheduling system via Google Calendar
  • Testimonials from past students
  • FAQ section with cancellation policy
  • Contact form for inquiries
  • Links to educational video content on YouTube and TikTok

Site Structure

  • Home (index.html) - Main landing page
  • About - Nathan's background and credentials
  • Services - Tutoring offerings including group sessions
  • Schedule - Google Calendar integration for booking
  • Contact/FAQ - Contact form and frequently asked questions

Site-Wide Components

  • Navigation Bar: Consistent header navigation enabling single-click access to all five pages
  • Header: Uniform header component with logo/name and navigation across all pages
  • Footer: Consistent footer including contact information, social media links, and HTML/CSS validation links (W3C Validator)

Page Design

Page 1: Home (index.html)

Attribute Details
Purpose Welcome visitors and provide quick navigation to key areas
Audience All visitors (students, parents, general public)
Content Hero image/banner, brief introduction to Nathan, subject highlights (Math, Physics, Python, Java), featured testimonial, call-to-action buttons
Data Entry No
Validations N/A
Interactive Elements Navigation bar, CTA buttons linking to Services and Schedule pages, testimonial carousel preview
Actions Click CTA → navigate to Services or Schedule; Click nav items → respective pages
Special Notes Should load quickly and make a strong first impression

Page 2: About

Attribute Details
Purpose Introduce Nathan, his background, qualifications, and teaching philosophy
Audience Prospective students and parents evaluating his credibility
Content Professional photo, biography, educational background, teaching experience, philosophy/approach to tutoring, links to YouTube and TikTok
Data Entry No
Validations N/A
Interactive Elements Social media icon links (YouTube, TikTok), possibly embedded video introduction
Actions Click social icons → open external links in new tab
Special Notes Tone should be personable and professional; builds trust

Page 3: Services

Attribute Details
Purpose Detail all tutoring services, subjects, pricing, and group session options
Audience Students and parents comparing tutoring options
Content Subject cards (Math, Physics, Python, Java) with descriptions, individual session rates, group session packages and pricing, what's included in each service
Data Entry No
Validations N/A
Interactive Elements Expandable/collapsible service cards, tabs or filters for Individual vs. Group sessions
Actions Click subject card → expand details; Click "Book Now" button → navigate to Schedule page
Special Notes Group sessions section should highlight family package deals clearly

Page 4: Schedule

Attribute Details
Purpose Allow visitors to view availability and book tutoring sessions
Audience Students and parents ready to schedule
Content Embedded Google Calendar showing availability, instructions for booking, cancellation policy summary with link to full policy
Data Entry Via embedded Google Calendar (external tool)
Validations Handled by Google Calendar
Interactive Elements Embedded Google Calendar widget, link to Contact page for questions
Actions Interact with calendar → book session through Google; Click policy link → navigate to FAQ section
Special Notes Calendar must be responsive; include fallback contact method

Page 5: Contact/FAQ

Attribute Details
Purpose Answer common questions and provide a way to reach Nathan directly
Audience Anyone with questions before booking
Content FAQ accordion (pricing, scheduling, cancellation policy, group sessions, subjects covered), contact form, direct contact information
Data Entry Yes - Contact form fields: Name, Email, Phone (optional), Subject dropdown, Message
Validations
  • Name: required, minimum 2 characters
  • Email: required, valid email format
  • Message: required, minimum 10 characters
  • Phone: optional, valid phone format if entered
Interactive Elements FAQ accordion (expand/collapse), form validation feedback, submit button
Actions Click FAQ item → expand/collapse answer; Submit form → validate inputs, show success/error message
Special Notes Cancellation policy should be prominently featured in FAQ
