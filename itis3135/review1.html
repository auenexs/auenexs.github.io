Evaluation Criteria

  1. Does the link lead directly to the page?

  2. Are there spaces or upper-cases in the file/folder names?

  3. Design Quality:
    • Does the page have good contrast?
    • Does the page have site colors and fonts using a custom CSS file?
    • Does it follow the CRAP principles?

  4. Does the page have a header, main, footer, and nav?

  5. Is there a branded h1?

  6. Does the main start with an h2?

  7. Does it have a tagline?

  8. Does it have all of the footer requirements?

  9. Does the page follow the specific requirements for the assignment?
